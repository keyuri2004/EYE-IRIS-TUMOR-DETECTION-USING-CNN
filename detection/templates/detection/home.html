Welcome to the Eye Iris Tumor Detector

* This tool uses advanced machine learning techniques to help detect iris tumors from uploaded eye images.

* Our mission is to provide accessible tools for early detection of eye health issues, helping to support healthcare professionals and patients alike.

Steps To Use

  1. Register and Login:Go to the Register page to create an account by providing your details.
  2. After logging in, navigate to the Home page.
  3. Click on the Upload Image button to select an eye image from your device.
  4. Ensure the image is clear and follows the format specified (e.g., .jpg, .png, etc.).
  5. After uploading, click the "Detect Tumor" button.
  6. The system will analyze the image and display whether a tumor is detected or not.
  7. Once done, click the Log Out button to safely exit your account.
